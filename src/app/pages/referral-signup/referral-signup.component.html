The Referral and Reward System (R&R system) is a system that allows for clients to earn-on-the-go by simply referring people to patronize any product/service offered by the smartHUB.

Any client can register with the smartHUB and obtain advert placement materials such as flyers (hard copy or digital) and redistribute to potential customers. Unique referral codes are then assigned to clients. Credits are awarded per customer converted to clients. Customers purchasing a product or accessing a service from the smartHUB may then use these referral codes during transactions in order to obtain discounts. At stipulated points in time (as determined by the number of credits earned), the clients who have amassed enough credits through referrals receive monetary reward equivalent to the referral credits gained.
