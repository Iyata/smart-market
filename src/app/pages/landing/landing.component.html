Our Vision

The smartMARKET has the drive to be “Africa’s number one digital market space and citadel for smart consumer goods”. This is in line with the parent company’s goal of “creatively bringing solutions to people’s everyday needs”.

Mission Statement

In achieving the vision of being the citadel for smart consumer goods, the smartMARKET has set out on a 7-point agenda in its mission. These are:

  • Never compromise product quality
  • Maintain a customer base across all of Africa
  • Physical presence in all cities in Africa
  • Collaborate with all wholesalers in African States
  • Partner with top vendors in Nigeria
  • Promote Nigerian/African products to the rest of the world
